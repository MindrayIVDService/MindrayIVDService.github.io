TLA Project Management Overview · 项目管理业务介绍

Core Objectives · 项目管理三大核心目标
01

Smooth Delivery · 顺利交付

Foundation · 交付基石

Ensure every TLA project is delivered on schedule and to standard.
保障每一个TLA项目按期、按标准顺利交付。

Enabled by risk management, timeline control and clear role responsibilities · 通过风险管理、时间管理、明确责任分工等手段共同保障
02

Talent Development · 人员培养

People · 人才梯队

Build TLA delivery talent across L3 regions.
面向三级区域，培养TLA交付人才梯队。

Four capability-coverage goals: Junior PM · Junior IT · CSE · CSA · 四项能力覆盖目标：初级PM、初级IT、CSE、CSA
03

Value Delivery · 价值交付

Beyond Install · 装机之外

Deliver customer value beyond the installation itself.
交付装机之外、真正惠及客户的价值。

e.g. smart-feature adoption, customer process efficiency & quality gains · 如智慧功能落地、客户流程效率与质量方向的优化

Role Responsibilities · 分层职责
PMO

Regional PMO · 区域PMO

6 Regions · 六大区域
01
Capability Building · 能力建设

Coach and develop L3-region TLA project managers to ensure regional teams have solid TLA project management capability.
带教培养三级区域TLA项目经理，确保区域团队具备TLA项目管理能力。

02
Regional Project Management · 区域项目管理

Coordinate cross-department resources; proactively identify and control regional project risks to keep delivery milestones and quality on track.
统筹跨部门资源协调，前置识别并管控区域项目风险，保障交付节点可控与项目质量。

03
Major Project Delivery · 重大项目交付

As a mid-level regional PM, lead the execution of major regional TLA projects and drive overall value delivery.
作为区域中级项目经理，主导重大TLA项目实施，统筹重大项目与价值交付。

PM

TLA Project Manager · TLA项目经理

Project Level · 项目级
01
Information Collection & Feedback · 信息收集反馈

Collect and consolidate regional project information; report to Regional/HQ PMO in a timely manner.
负责区域内项目信息收集汇总，及时反馈区域/总部PMO。

02
Project Meetings · 项目会议组织

Set up the project team and hold regular communication meetings; kick-off and closure meetings are mandatory.
成立项目团队，按期召开沟通会；立项会、结项会必须组织召开。

03
On-site Organization · 现场交付组织

Arrange manpower allocation reasonably; prepare the project plan and team grouping in advance.
合理安排人员分工，提前制定项目计划和人员分组。

04
Progress Reporting · 进度汇报

Post a daily work summary in the project group chat; send a weekly delivery report by email.
项目群每日发布工作小结，邮件发送项目交付周报。

05
Deliverables & Platform Update · 输出物与平台刷新

Organize project deliverables and keep the International TLA Project Management Platform up to date.
整理输出项目交付物，及时刷新国际TLA项目管理平台信息。
