Data ContractReal calculation logic from RTS monthly report
Self-Coverage = non-HQ (Global TC) sessions / total sessions, target ≥90%
Scheduled Rate = (Global+Regional+Channel+National TC) sessions / total sessions, target ≥50%
MCSR Rate = trainees who obtained MCSR / total trainees (pass=Y or N), target ≥70%
CS/RTS Score sub-item mapping (from update_dashboard.py's real parse_score() logic — the same field code means a different metric per role):
  CSE (target 60): S1=MCSR Coverage · S3=Technical Assessment · S4=Technical Seminar · S6=Cases Submitted · S7=Training Delivered · S9=MCST Coverage
  Service KA (target 20): S1=Technical Assessment · S2=Recurring Business Assessment · S4=Technical Seminar · S5=Recurring Business Course · S9=MCSR Coverage
  RTS/RTSE (target 20): S1=Technical Assessment · S3=MCST Coverage · S5=Recurring Business Course · S6=Cases Submitted · S7=Training Delivered

Data Contract
Not Started: status = not started
At Risk: status = in progress, AND (overdue tasks >5 OR days since last study >14)
On Track: status = in progress, and neither condition above applies
Completed: status = completed
These thresholds were set against this sample and are not an official HQ standard yet — confirm with HQ RTS before go-live.
